IN THE NEWS

Study Points to Causes Behind Age-Linked Memory Loss

Researchers are looking at connections between memory declines in older age and brain networks less able to maintain their specialized funcitions. This research could point to indicators in the brain that could predict pending memory problems. (philly.com)

Chocolate Restores Age-Related Memory Loss

"After three months, the group that got the supercharged flavanol drink showed greatly improved performance on the test of memory, while the low-flavanol group showed no such improvement." (Sci-Tech Today)



DID YOU KNOW?

That the simple act of walking through a doorway can cause you to go suddenly blank? We've all walked into a room and suddenly realized that we can't remember why we needed to be there. Researchers have found that the simple act of walking through a doorway can cause a memory lapse when trying to recall a purpose that was initiated in another room before.
