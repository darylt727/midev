Memory Apps

Doing an app search for "memory" produces any number of results for memory games to choose from (this general search produces results for computer memory too, so check the app descriptions). Here are some 4-star rated apps:

Games
Memory, developed by Berni Mobile is a puzzle game that challenges your memory by matching pairs of cards. The game has different levels of difficulty for all ages. Android
Match Up: Exercise your Memory, by Magma Mobile, is a card game designed for two people, but has single-player mode available. Match pairs of cards in as little moves as possible, with different levels of difficulty for all ages. Android | IOS
Brain Games-Fast Memory Games - from APPIFY, this app has several games built into one to test memory and concentration.
Memory Matches, by IDC Projects, is a card matching game to test and improve your memory, with different card themes that allow you to switch things up. Android | IOS

GTD (Getting Things Done)
Remember the Milk - An free online to-do list that also has a phone app. The app has some minor restrictions (free users can only sync between their smartphone and online account once every 24 hours), but overall, it is a very useful tool for remembering things on the go. You can tag, prioritize and set due dates for the items on your to do list and always have that list with you on your phone. Android | IOS
Todoist is another highly-rated to do list app that syncs with a free, online account, with many priority and due date capabilities, among other features. Android | IOS

Trainers
Memory Trainer, developed by Urbian, "works out your spatial and working memory, focus, chunking and concentration skills" and tracks your progress.

IN THE NEWS

Study Points to Causes Behind Age-Linked Memory Loss

Researchers are looking at connections between memory declines in older age and brain networks less able to maintain their specialized funcitions. This research could point to indicators in the brain that could predict pending memory problems. (philly.com)

Chocolate Restores Age-Related Memory Loss

"After three months, the group that got the supercharged flavanol drink showed greatly improved performance on the test of memory, while the low-flavanol group showed no such improvement." (Sci-Tech Today)



DID YOU KNOW?

That the simple act of walking through a doorway can cause you to go suddenly blank? We've all walked into a room and suddenly realized that we can't remember why we needed to be there. Researchers have found that the simple act of walking through a doorway can cause a memory lapse when trying to recall a purpose that was initiated in another room before.
